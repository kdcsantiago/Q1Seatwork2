Noli me Tangere Characters

Image Name Description
Juan Crisostomo Ibarra y Magsalin Crisostomo Ibarra was a young San Diegan student. Following his father's death, he returned to his birthplace to construct a good school, only to be faced with various difficulties from local officials.
Padre Damaso Padre Damaso is Maria Clara's father and the previous town curate of San Diego. The father worked hard to keep his daughter's marriage to Crisostomo Ibarra apart for the sake of Maria Clara.
Maria Clara de los Santos Maria Clara de los Santos was the fiancee of Crisostomo Ibarra and the daughter of Capitan Tiago. Maria Clara encountered various obstacles to their betrothal once Ibarra returned to San Diego.
Capitan Tiago Capitan Tiago, Maria Clara's father, was a powerful businessman in San Diego. Capitan Tiago strained to fulfill the friars' demands by marrying his daughter to Crisostomo Ibarra.
Elias Elias was a wanted man residing in San Diego. After enduring one tragedy after another, he began fighting to change society. After Crisostomo Ibarra saved his life, he kept assisting the young guy, saving him on several occasions.
